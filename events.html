Community Events

From holiday celebrations to summer pool days, Glenridge brings neighbors together with events throughout the year.

Holiday Celebrations

From Thanksgiving gatherings to Christmas luminaries, we come together to celebrate the holiday season as a community.

Easter Egg Hunt

An annual favorite for the kids — our Easter egg hunt brings families together for a fun-filled spring morning.

Movie Night

Grab your blankets and popcorn for outdoor movie nights under the stars — a neighborhood favorite.

Swim Team

Our summer swim team brings competitive fun and teamwork to the pool season for kids of all ages.

Neighborhood block parties and picnics in our community common areas — great food, games, and company.
