Pool Gate Access

Choose how your household accesses the pool gate. You can register a phone (using the Glenridge Pool mobile app), request an RFID key card, or use both. The pool admin must approve each method before it will open the gate.

📱 Phone Access

Register your iPhone or Android phone. Once registered, use the Glenridge Pool app to tap the gate open.

💳 RFID Key Card

An RFID key card opens the gate with a tap — no phone needed. Each family may request up to 2 key cards. Cards are registered at the pool entry gate — bring your card (or yourself) to the pool and a staff member will complete the registration.

📋 How to register your key card:
  • Need a new card? Request one below. The pool admin will have it ready for you.
  • Already have a card? Bring it to the pool gate entry — a staff member will scan and register it to your account.

⚠ Cards are not active until registered in person at the pool.
